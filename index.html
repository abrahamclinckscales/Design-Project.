NBA

The NBA is a basketball organization for talented atheletes. Nba stands for National Basketball Association. The NBA has been around for years, it was founded june 6, 1946 in new york. The NBA is only found in America but there are other basketball organizations around the world that you may see.Each year they do a draft to get new players on each team.
